what we do

Four ways to gather

Every Design Lounge event is built around the same idea: good company, low pressure, and conversations that actually go somewhere.

social mixers

Drinks & Designers

Our signature happy hour. Cocktails, sunset views, and a room full of UX, UI, and product designers ready to talk shop — or anything but. Gentle icebreakers help the introverts in (we see you).

  • Casual evening mixers at NYC's loveliest bars
  • Designers, design leaders & friendly recruiters
  • No name tags, no pitches — just good conversation
coworking days

Designers Cowork

Bring your laptop and your latest project. We take over a bright, comfortable space for a day of heads-down work, side-by-side critique, and very good coffee.

  • Quiet focus blocks with optional crit circles
  • Portfolio feedback from working designers
  • A standing antidote to working-from-home alone
immersive days

Designers Hack

One day, one brief, small teams, big swings. Our hackathon days pair designers across disciplines to concept, prototype, and present — with crits from senior design leaders to close it out.

  • Full-day immersive design sprints
  • Teams matched across UX, UI, brand & motion
  • Demos, feedback, and a celebratory toast
fast friends

Speed Networking

Like speed dating, but for your design network. Short, structured rounds of one-on-one conversation — every bell means a new face, a new portfolio, a new connection worth keeping.

  • Timed rounds, fresh conversation prompts
  • Meet 10+ designers in a single evening
  • Swap contacts, follow up over coffee later
about us

A lounge, not a lecture

Design Lounge is an event group for New York's design community — the UX researchers, product designers, brand thinkers, and pixel-pushers who make this city's screens worth looking at.

We believe the best career moves start as casual conversations. So we make rooms that feel like a friend's living room: warm, unhurried, and a little bit fancy. Come for the cocktails, stay for the people.
