Four waysto gather

(01 — 04)

Every Design Lounge event is built around the same idea: good company, low pressure, and conversations that actually go somewhere.

01
Drinks & Designers
Our signature happy hour. Cocktails, sunset views, and a room full of designers ready to talk shop — or anything but. No name tags, no pitches.
02
Designers Cowork
Bring your laptop and your latest project. A bright space for a day of heads-down work, side-by-side critique, and very good coffee.
03
Designers Hack
One day, one brief, small teams, big swings. Designers paired across disciplines to concept, prototype, and present — with crits from senior leaders.
04
Speed Networking
Like speed dating, but for your design network. Short, structured rounds — every bell means a new face, a new portfolio, a new connection worth keeping.
About the lounge

A lounge,not a lecture

Design Lounge is an event group for New York's design community — the UX researchers, product designers, brand thinkers, and pixel-pushers who make this city's screens worth looking at.

We believe the best career moves start as casual conversations. So we make rooms that feel like a friend's living room: warm, unhurried, and a little bit fancy. Come for the cocktails, stay for the people.
