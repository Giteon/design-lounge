what we do

Our Events

Design Lounge runs events for every mood — from laid-back happy hours to focused work sessions and creative collisions.

The flagship event: NYC Drinks + Designers. Fresh cocktails, a great room, and UX, UI, product & graphic designers ready to talk shop — or anything but. It runs roughly monthly and starts at 6:30 PM.

  • Recurring evenings at Bar Hugo & Azul at Hotel Hugo
  • All digital designers — plus a few friendly recruiters
  • Fun icebreakers and good conversation/li>

Bring your laptop, your deadline, and your appetite for a great coffee. Coworking sessions put you in a room of fellow designers — quiet enough to focus, social enough to spark something.

  • A productive setting with the right kind of company
  • Great for freelancers, remote workers, and side-project types
  • Occasional casual critique and peer feedback

A structured evening to build, prototype, and ship — with other designers pushing alongside you. Hack nights are equal parts creative energy and accountability, wrapped up in a few hours.

  • Themed briefs or open build nights
  • Team up or go solo — both work
  • End-of-night share-out and feedback

The format that packs a month of introductions into one evening. Round-robin style, everyone meets everyone — and you leave with a stack of real conversations instead of a handful of business cards.

  • Structured rounds, no pressure to pitch
  • Great for job-seekers, hirers, and collaborators
  • Ends with open mingling so the good conversations keep going
about us

A lounge,
not a lecture

Design Lounge is a New York meetup for everyone working in design — UX, UI, product, graphic, and every other corner of digital design. We're 1,300 members strong!

We believe the best career moves start as casual conversations. So we create a space that's warm, unhurried, and a little bit fancy.
